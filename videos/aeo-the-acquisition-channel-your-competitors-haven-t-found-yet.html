AEO: The Acquisition Channel Your Competitors Haven't Found Yet

AI is already recommending products to buyers. The question isn't whether this channel is real — a 2025 study across 329 brands found LLM-referred traffic converts at 2.47%, ahead of Google Ads at 1.82% and miles above Meta at 0.52%.

The question is whether those buyers are finding your products, or your competitor's.

Right now, it comes down to one thing most e-commerce owners have never touched: structured data and enriched metadata that AI agents can actually read.

This isn't a content strategy pivot. It's a catalog hygiene problem with a direct revenue consequence. If your product data is messy, inconsistent, or incomplete across your Shopify store and your marketplace listings — AI skips you. Full stop.

The owner who invests two weeks cleaning this up now is positioned on a channel growing faster than paid social, before it gets crowded and expensive.

That window is open. It won't stay open long.
