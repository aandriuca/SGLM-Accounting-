Shopify Sidekick Voice AI — The Half It Can't Answer

Shopify Sidekick's voice feature is genuinely impressive. Ask which products drive your highest AOV out loud — and get a buildable report in seconds.

But here's the thing: it answers exactly half the question.

Sidekick has perfect visibility inside Shopify. It has zero visibility into your Amazon payouts, your FBA fee stack, your landed cost per unit, or whether that high-AOV product is actually your most profitable one once the full picture is in.

This isn't a knock on the tool. It's a knock on assuming the tool has everything it needs.

The owners who win with AI are the ones who already know which numbers to trust — because when the inputs are incomplete, the recommendations are too.

Use every smart tool available. Just make sure someone's accountable for the numbers underneath them.
