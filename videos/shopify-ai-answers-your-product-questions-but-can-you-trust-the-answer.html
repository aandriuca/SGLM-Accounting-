Shopify AI Answers Your Product Questions — But Can You Trust the Answer?

Shopify's Summer 2025 Edition just made your data way more accessible. Ask a plain-English question, get a live report instantly. Voice-enabled. No SQL. No analyst.

That part is genuinely impressive.

Here's the part nobody's talking about: your highest average-order-value product is NOT automatically your most profitable one. It might also carry your worst return rate and your highest pick-pack cost. The AI will show you the number. It won't tell you whether the number is good news or a slow bleed.

Faster access to the question is not the same as knowing what the answer means.

The owners who are actually pulling ahead aren't the ones with the fastest dashboards — they're the ones who understand what sits behind every figure their store surfaces.
